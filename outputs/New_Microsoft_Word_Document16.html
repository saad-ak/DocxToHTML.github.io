Must-Watch Bollywood Hindi Movies to Stream

Here are some must-watch Bollywood Hindi movies, each representing a different genre and catering to various tastes:

1. Gully Boy (2019) A Raging Musical Drama

If you're a fan of rap music and urban struggles, Gully Boy is a must-watch. Directed by Zoya Akhtar, this film tells the story of an aspiring street rapper from Mumbai. Starring Ranveer Singh and Alia Bhatt, Gully Boy gives viewers an inside look into the underground rap scene in India and addresses social issues through powerful music.

2. Dangal (2016) The Inspirational Sports Drama

Dangal, starring Aamir Khan, is a heartwarming film based on the true story of wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat and his daughters, who go on to become world-class wrestlers. The film not only inspires viewers with its message of determination and grit but also highlights the importance of breaking gender stereotypes.

3. KabhiKhushiKabhieGham (2001) The Family Saga

If you're in the mood for an epic family drama, KabhiKhushiKabhieGham is an absolute classic. Directed by Karan Johar, this film brings together a star-studded ensemble, including Amitabh Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan, Kajol, and HrithikRoshan. The story of family bonds, love, and loss remains a favorite for many Bollywood fans.

4. Zindagi Na MilegiDobara (2011) A Journey of Friendship and Self-Discovery

This film is perfect for those who love to travel and explore new horizons. Directed by Zoya Akhtar, Zindagi Na MilegiDobara follows the journey of three friends who go on a road trip across Spain. Filled with scenic beauty, fun, and emotional moments, the film explores themes of love, life, and friendship.

5. Queen (2013) A Powerful Tale of Self-Discovery

Queen, starring KanganaRanaut, is the story of a young woman who embarks on a solo honeymoon trip to Europe after her marriage falls apart. The film is a beautiful journey of self-discovery and empowerment, making it a must-watch for anyone looking for a movie with a strong female protagonist.

6. Lagaan (2001) The Epic Sports Drama

Lagaan is a historical sports drama set in the British colonial era. The story revolves around a group of Indian villagers who challenge the British rulers to a cricket match to avoid a heavy tax. Directed by AshutoshGowariker and starring Aamir Khan, Lagaan received global recognition and was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film.

7. Andhadhun (2018) A Thriller Like No Other

If you enjoy suspense and thrillers, Andhadhun is a film that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Directed by SriramRaghavan, the film tells the story of a blind pianist who becomes embroiled in a murder mystery. With its gripping storyline, unexpected twists, and stellar performances by AyushmannKhurrana and Tabu, this film is a must-see for thriller fans.

8. Gully Boy (2019) A Musical Journey of Hope

Directed by Zoya Akhtar, Gully Boy explores the underground rap scene of Mumbai through the story of a young man aspiring to become a rapper. Featuring a fantastic soundtrack with hits like "Apna Time Aayega," the film celebrates the power of music and self-expression.
